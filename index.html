The AKATSUKI

Organisation

Akatsuki is a powerful and iconic group of anime villains, with members who can take on elite ninjas and Tailed Beasts.The Akatsuki is a secretive and highly dangerous organization composed of rogue shinobi who abandoned their respective villages. Their ultimate goal is to create a new world order, often employing extreme methods such as war, manipulation, and the capture of powerful tailed beasts known as Bijuu.
